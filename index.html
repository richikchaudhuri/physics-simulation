Physics Sandbox

A real-time 3D physics engine written in Rust, compiled to WebAssembly, and rendered with Three.js at 60 fps.

Four interactive simulations — elastic collisions, an N-body gravity disk, chaotic double pendulums and a mass-spring cloth — stream their state zero-copy from WASM linear memory straight into GPU-instanced geometry every frame.
